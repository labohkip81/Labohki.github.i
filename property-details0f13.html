Gigiri - Off Camellia Close

Gigiri
5 Bedroom Villa
Kshs 130m
Facebook Twitter Google +
Expand All
 

The property is in a serene environment benefiting from excellent accessibility in the very much sort out part of Gigiri. The spacious 5 bedroom house comprises of 3 levels. The entrance opens up to the guestroom which is en-suite, cloak room, a country style fireplace, well-lit spacious lounge cum dining with a lovely view of the garden from the balcony. On this same level there is a fitted kitchen, pantry and laundry area.

Going up  there is a family room and 4 other bedrooms 2 which are en-suite including the master bedroom with a balcony and the other 2 sharing.

The lowest level holds yet another family room with access to the beautiful well maintained garden, cloak room and a sauna.

Finishes comprise of wooden parquet flooring and ceramic tiles in wet areas.
